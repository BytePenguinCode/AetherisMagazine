About Aetheris Magazine

Aetheris derives from the latin word "aether", which historically refers to the pure essence that the gods breathed, often associated with the sky or the celestial realm. This name encapsulates the magazine's mission to explore the lofty heights of scientific discovery and innovation, bridging the gap between complex scientific concepts and the curious minds eager to understand them.
